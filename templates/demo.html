Ipaymatics History

IPaymatics- India’s largest e-commerce website - an ultimate destination for college students to help them in saving money. Ipaymatics is a basic type of savings account that allows you to deposit money, keep it safe, and withdraw money, all while earning interest of 1.5%. Interest rates of various banks are less than 1 percent annually which makes attracted towards IPaymatics.

The other astounding scheme from Ipaymatics’s Savings account is that the account holder can refer others to join Ipaymatics and will get an additional 50% of the amount of the new account holder’s deposit. This scheme is called Referral Scheme.
